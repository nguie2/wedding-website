Once upon a time

How two souls found their way

&

Tommy and Sifa met when they were very young — Sifa was only fourteen. They started as best friends, and over the years that friendship slowly, quietly turned into love.

Before Sifa left for South Africa, they had already built a special connection — and somehow life always kept bringing them back to each other. No matter where the years took them, they always found their way home to one another.

It truly feels like they were meant for each other — as if their souls had already met long before they did. Their story has always been about timing, destiny, friendship, and a love that never faded.

Leur histoire a toujours été faite de destin, d'amitié, et d'un amour qui n'a jamais fané.
